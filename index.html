Vision & Mission

Vision

The vision of UAD is to be an excellent and innovative university that integrates Islamic values and serves the interests of the nation and the people.

Mission

  • implement Al-Islam and Muhammadiyah (AIK) values in all aspects of its activities;
  • advance science, technology and art through education, research and community service;
  • establish and develop cooperation and collaboration at the local, national and international levels; and
  • organize good university governance.
